# Lehman Brothers — Senior Programmer Analyst, Equity Front End Systems

## Context

Full-time employee on the Equity Front End Systems team at Lehman Brothers, building real-time decision-support systems for equity traders. More formal environment than later consulting engagements — less directly embedded with traders, but hands-on with deployed systems in production across multiple desks.

## Contributions

### Risk Management Prototype
- what: rapid application prototype for a risk management system
- stack: C++, Teleuse, Sybase
- impact: established the design and gained stakeholder buy-in; led directly to the full Real-Time Risk Management System

### Real-Time Risk Management System
- what: real-time risk management system calculating option pricing models, sensitivities, and aggregate calculations from real-time price changes, distributed across processes and machines
- stack: C++, Sybase, X Windows
- impact: deployed and used across several equity trading desks; calculated real-time Greek option metrics (delta, gamma, theta, etc.) from live price and position changes, displayed in a flashing spreadsheet-like X Windows interface; event-based distributed C++ architecture scalable to firm-wide level — ahead of its time for 1993

## Responsibilities

### Production Support
- scope: deployed trading systems across multiple Lehman equity desks
- Ongoing support and maintenance for the risk management system in production across multiple desks
