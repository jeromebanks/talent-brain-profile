# Patents

## Monitoring Performance of Applications in a Distributed Environment
Co-inventors: James C. Chong, Joseph L. Chan, Tushar M. Patel, Jean-Jacques Heler, Chi Hong So, Arthur Tsang, Robert S. Lam, Raymond Chow, Jerome D. Banks, Christopher M. Zychowski, Henry Tang. Originally assigned to Cyanea Systems Corp.; reassigned to IBM Corp. following IBM's July 2004 acquisition of Cyanea.

Invention conceived and originally filed while at Cyanea Systems (2002–2004); IBM continued the family for over a decade after acquiring Cyanea. Four granted US patents:
- **US7870244B2** — filed 2003-06-25, granted 2011-01-11 (the original filing; assignment to Cyanea recorded 2003-11-17)
- **US8037205B2** — continuation, filed 2008-09-23, granted 2011-10-11
- **US9053220B2** — continuation, filed 2011-07-15, granted 2015-06-09
- **US9678964B2** — continuation, filed 2015-05-04, granted 2017-06-13

Covers application performance monitoring in distributed/enterprise environments — drill-down diagnostics from server groups down to individual threads. This is the patent-filing counterpart to the Application Performance Monitoring work at Cyanea. See [Cyanea Systems](../experience/cyanea.md).

## Dynamic Information Streams in a Social Network Platform
Co-inventors: David Friedman, Girish Lingappa, Alexy Khrabrov, Jerome Banks, Andras Benke, Nemanja Spasojevic, Adithya Shricharan Rao, Ding Zhou. Assignee: Klout, Inc. (now Lithium Technologies LLC).

Filed as two related applications, both since abandoned (never issued as a granted patent):
- **US20130318156A1** — filed 2013-05-23
- **US20140040377A1** — filed 2013-09-24 (continuation of the above; claims priority to a 2012-05-23 provisional)

Describes a system for automatically surfacing content from topical experts and influencers into a user's social feed, based on inferred interests — the patent-filing counterpart to the topic-assignment and influence-scoring work at Klout. See [Klout — Topic Thunder](../experience/klout.md#topic-thunder--topic-assignment).
